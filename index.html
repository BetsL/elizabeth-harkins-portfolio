Backstory

My journey into tech started with tinkering with my classroom website. After I began playing around with basic HTML and CSS, I was hooked! I became curious about how to build dynamic materials for my classroom and personal interests.

When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I had found something that gave me the freedom + versatility I was looking for in my work. I thoroughly enjoy front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

I continue to be excited to build and develop tools for use with my classroom community.
